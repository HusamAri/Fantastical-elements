# PRODUCT.md — Curated Chaos / Fantastical Elements

register: brand

## Product Purpose

The Curated Chaos universe site: an end-to-end interactive guide for visual language building in
cinematic universes, and the public home of **Fragments of Real People (FORP)** — 4–5 minute
standalone action episodes with fantastical elements. The site is itself a piece of the cinema:
a scroll-driven 3D experience where the universe's physics (density, light, pressure) respond to
the reader's movement. Design IS the product.

The locked site concept is **THE LIVING ARCHIVE — Night Wing**
(`playbooks/chaos-site-style/STYLE-CONCEPT.md`): dark halls where exhibits wake under a moving
curator's light as you scroll. Any design work on the site conforms to that concept.

## Users

- Viewers arriving from Instagram / Behance / episode links — they should feel the universe
  before they understand it.
- Creative directors, motion designers, AI filmmakers studying the method — they read deeper:
  playbooks, bibles, breakdowns.
- Collaborators and "fragments" (the real people behind characters) — they find themselves
  treated with restraint and dignity.

## Brand

Curated Chaos — **"Beauty in Contrast."** A dark editorial archive with cinematic atmosphere.
Grounded photorealism; containment over destruction; pressure over spectacle; physics over
fantasy. The hummingbird emblem is discovered, not announced — never the loudest thing on any
surface. The universe's palette (Istanbul blue hour, sodium amber) lives inside artwork and
film frames; the BRAND surfaces around them obey the codex tokens (see DESIGN.md).

## Tone

Implies rather than states ("imply, don't state" is law). Quiet authority. One thought per
heading. Short body text; silence and negative space carry as much meaning as words. Never
wellness language, never hype adjectives, never stated metaphors.

## Anti-references (never this)

- Neon cyberpunk, RGB glitch, holographic UI, teal-and-orange grading
- Glow effects, gradients, recolored emblems, sticker-style over-branding
- Text overload, default template aesthetics, glitch spam
- Fast decorative motion; anything that moves without narrative purpose
- Victimhood or hype narratives; exclamation-point energy

## Strategic principles

1. Restraint carries the spectacle: the "amazing" comes from light, density, grain, depth and
   timing — codex-legal physics, never banned effects.
2. Motion is narrative: the signature movement is slow circular drift (motion.orbit.slow);
   every animation answers "what does the viewer understand now?"
3. Locked surfaces are readable (WCAG AA non-negotiable); free surfaces (covers, dividers,
   art plates) may let mood win.
4. The scroll is the camera: scrolling the site is a dolly move through the universe; text
   locations are the cut points.
5. Every asset is produced under one-shot discipline (see the FORP playbook) — planned,
   preflighted, approved; no throwaway generations.
