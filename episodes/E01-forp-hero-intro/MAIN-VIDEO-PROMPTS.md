# E01 — MAIN VIDEO: two 15-second single-shot prompts (director's own trial)

For the director's experiment: pack the whole N=17 storyboard into ONE 15-second generation
(Seedance 2.0 and Omni Flash), rather than the chained per-clip method. Both apply the CORRECTED
**solid-disc** rule (V13): the discs are thrown glass objects — never flame, energy, or magic.
Beat order compresses: cast → shatter → twin-disc throw → interrupt → Flame Inside → Ancient
Sunlight sun-burst → settle. Emotional spine = the throw + the sun-burst; drop the interrupt first
if the model blurs too many beats.

## PROMPT A — Seedance 2.0 (15s · 1080p · genre epic · audio ON · single continuous take)
Single continuous 15-second cinematic take, no cuts. A lean bearded man (late 30s, receding
hairline, high forehead) in a long high-collar black robe with bronze trim and a taupe mantle-cape
stands in a deep gold-and-black void filled with suspended shards of shattered black glass. Heavy
35mm analog grain, practical light, photographed — NOT CG.
0–3s: calm; he lifts one open hand and a solid smoked-glass disc — dark glass, faint concentric
grooves, thin gold rim-light — spins up off his palm and hovers, rotating. Camera slow push-in.
3–6s: he snaps his hand down and the whole world SHATTERS into black glass that blasts toward
camera; deep sub-bass boom.
6–9s: he turns and, from both hands, flicks two solid glass discs low and forward like skipping
stones; they spin fast, spiral around each other, collide and burst into embered glass. Camera arcs
WITH the throw.
9–12s: a hooded attacker lunges and strikes the discs apart; he staggers — then fire ignites from
within his chest and sweeps outward along his arms.
12–15s: he raises both arms and calls down a blinding sun-burst that whites out the frame, then the
glare falls to reveal him standing calm in slow-drifting golden glass-dust.
Motion: smooth, weighty, real physics — shards tumble, embers drift, mantle flares; every element
moves, nothing frozen. Camera: one flowing gimbal move that tracks the action. Sound: low drone →
glass-shatter crack → spinning-disc whoosh → hard clash → fire whoosh → half-second silence → vast
low sun-burst boom. Grade: grainy gold-and-black void, warm ember accents. The discs are SOLID
thrown glass — not a fireball, not energy, not magic casting.

## PROMPT B — Omni Flash / Gemini (paste as-is; orbit-led camera, a second "take")
A 15-second unbroken cinematic shot with visible film grain, photographed and practical — never CG.
Setting: an infinite black void split by a single tall gold light-panel and filled with slow-
floating shards of broken black glass. A lean, bearded man in a long black high-collar robe and
taupe mantle stands at centre. He lifts a hand and a solid disc of dark smoked glass with a gold-lit
edge spins to life above his palm, hovering and turning. With one sharp gesture the reality around
him cracks and explodes into glass while the camera orbits him and the shards blast outward. He
pivots and throws two solid glass discs from both hands, low like skipping stones; they spiral
around one another, collide and burst in a spray of embered glass. A hooded figure rushes in and
shatters the discs in mid-air; he reels, and fire kindles inside his chest and pours outward across
his arms. He throws his head back and summons a colossal sunburst — pure white light floods and
blinds the frame — then the glare settles to reveal him standing quietly as golden dust falls. A
continuous camera follows the motion; weighty, realistic physics; every shard and ember stays in
motion. Audio: deep drone, glass shatter, spinning-disc whoosh, hard clash, roaring fire, one beat
of silence, then a vast low sun-burst boom. The discs must stay SOLID thrown glass objects — never
flame, energy, or magic.
