## Signature weapon
**Razor-glass discs** — spinning chakram-like discs, fire-ember edged. Thrown by spinning off his
first three fingers like a bowling ball. His powers are an original disc mechanic (thrown / spun /
dual-wielded) — NOT a static shield or any franchise look. Role: **APC (Ability Power Carry)**.

## Abilities
- **Basic attack — Razor Throw.** Throws the disc in hand, one at a time, alternating LEFT then
  RIGHT hand, spun off the first three fingers like a bowling ball. Ranged single-target.
- **Skill 1 (Q) — Twin Spiral.** Spins a disc from BOTH hands at once; the two discs empower each
  other, spiralling around one another along a linear path, and **explode when they reach their
  range**. Target **burns 5s**.
- **Skill 2 (W) — Cheeky Jump.** Spins two discs from each hand in TWO different directions. He
  **jumps to one disc and holds it**; kinetic force from the touched disc erupts into the air,
  **damaging everything in between**, and **teleports the enemy to the second disc's location**.
- **Skill 3 (E) — Locker Disc.** Spins a disc behind his back — this one is **smoke/shadow** (a dark
  disc). He **aims a spot on the ground with his eyes**; when he crushes the dark disc in his hand,
  the designated area **burns**, damaging anyone there and **locking them in place for 2s**.
- **Skill 4 (Passive/Transform) — Flame Inside.** He **fire-forges when he takes damage**; at a
  threshold his inner fire becomes visible — he looks **shinier and smokier** (like the glowing disc
  edges in the reference). Lasts **10s**. While active, all skills are enhanced:
  - Basic attacks: **2× power**.
  - Skill 1: **3 spiral discs** (not 2).
  - Skill 3: lock **5s** (not 2).
  - Skill 2: **jumps more than once** — the second jump goes in the direction past the second
    destination.
- **Ultimate (R) — Ancient Sunlight.** Usable **only in Flame Inside** mode. Every hit applies a
  **flame stack** to the enemy (each disc = 1 stack). On the **5th stack**, an **ancient light from
  the sun burns a flash onto the target** — deadly, especially below a certain HP threshold (an
  execute).

## Visual/production notes
- The **disc** is the through-line of every action beat — spinning, thrown, dual-wielded, exploding,
  shadow-locking. Each ability has a distinct disc behaviour to keyframe (spiral, jump-teleport,
  shadow ground-lock, flame-enhanced variants).
- **Flame Inside** is his transformation/"super" state — the visual escalation beat for the episode
  climax and a strong site moment.
- Continuity: costume + face locked via `HusamHero`; disc-fire aesthetic + grain locked via the same
